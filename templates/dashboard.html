          How it works
          • Your employability score updates automatically whenever you upload or remove a document, or add/remove a skill — no manual refresh needed.
          • Skills are scored by market demand weight. High-demand technical skills (Python, AWS, AI) contribute significantly more than commodity skills (Typing, Filing).
          • Setting a target role helps the Market Competitiveness dimension benchmark your profile against your actual goal.
          • The more independent supporting documents you upload (certificates, references, transcripts), the higher your Documentation Strength and Evidence Credibility scores can go.
          • Only one person's documents can be associated with an account. Removing all documents will reset your account to onboarding on next sign-in.
